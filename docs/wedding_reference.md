> *We should have connected sooner than we did.*
> 
> *In 2021, when Angel was in law school, several of Gavin’s friends visited Tuscaloosa — Gavin had other plans and had to miss the trip.*
>

> *Gavin made the first big move, inviting Angel to his birthday celebration in Huntsville. The two of us talked for three hours before Angel had to go. Gavin once again made a big move, asking Angel to go on another date in Birmingham the next week. We have been together ever since.*

> *So yeah, we should have connected sooner than we did. But we connected exactly when we were supposed to.*

## The Bridge — How They Got Connected

**Cody Cook** was Gavin’s college roommate for two years.
**Alyssa Cook** was Angel’s law school roommate (3L year).
Cody married Alyssa in May 2023 — Gavin and Angel were both in that wedding but only chatted briefly.
Four months later, at a wedding shower in September 2023, Alyssa decided she had a match for Gavin. Within an hour, Gavin texted Angel for the first time.

That’s why Cody and Alyssa are both in the wedding party — and why Alyssa’s title is officially **“Bridesmaid AND Matchmaker.”**

**Timeline:**

- 2021 — Near-miss in Tuscaloosa (Gavin had other plans)
- May 2023 — Both in the Cook wedding, brief chat, no spark yet
- September 2023 — Wedding shower, Alyssa makes the call, first text within the hour
- Birthday in Huntsville — three-hour conversation
- The next week — second date in Birmingham
- Then “medium distance” → home together in Birmingham
- Tennessee vs. Alabama games are the only real test (and they survive it)
